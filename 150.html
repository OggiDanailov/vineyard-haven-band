150TH ANNIVERSARY GALA CONCERT

August 9, 2018

* 7:30 p.m. The Tabernacle, Oak Bluffs Free admission *

Reception to follow

John Washbrook, an Island actor best known for starring as 12-year-old Ken McLauglin in the 1950s CBS show "My Friend Flicka," will perform a staged reading of Lincoln’s Gettysburg Address, with musical accompaniment, to honor the Band’s legacy.

The Band especially encourages past members to join us, either as guest performers or in the audience. To play in the gala, or to join the Band for any or all of the season’s concerts, please contact Jeri Larson for rehearsal information: 203-206-7706 or jeriwlarson@gmail.com
