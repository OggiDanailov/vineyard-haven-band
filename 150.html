KICK-OFF FOR OUR 150th ANNIVERSARY YEAR :

HOLIDAY POP-UP CONCERT, SATURDAY 16, at 1:00 pm - Main Street, Vineyard Haven (in front of Bobby B's / Bernie's)

The Vineyard Haven Band will be giving an outdoor Holiday Concert to kick-off our 150th Season. We encourage any island musicians, especially student musicians, to join us in playing familiar holiday tunes, including a Chanukah song.

This off-season concert is aimed at raising the awareness in the community of the unique history of the band. We are one of the three oldest, continuously performing town bands in the United States. We were founded by eighteen Confederate and Union soldiers shortly after the end of the Civil War in 1868. What a wonderful heritage to celebrate !!!

The Mansion House Hotel will be hosting a post-concert party for the band and attendees with hot cider and light refreshments.

We are planning a major celebration for our 150th season in 2018. Stay tuned for updates as we put together a summer to remember.

If you have ever been a musician, consider joining us. If you would like to support the Band, please consider placing an ad in our commemorative ad journal, sponsoring a concert, or contributing to our operations as we mount an island-wide celebration.

For sponsorship opportunities and rates for our 150th Anniversary Concert Journal, please contact Belinda Eichler: bseichler@aol.com
