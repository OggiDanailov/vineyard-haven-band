VINEYARD HAVEN BAND

Established 1868 on the island of Martha's Vineyard, Massachusetts, the Vineyard Haven Band is the second-oldest community band in the U.S. Every summer since right after the Civil War, we have brought together Vineyarders and visitors for free, public band-in-the-park concerts every week. Julie Schilling is our current Music Director.
