JOIN US!

PLAY AN INSTRUMENT? JOIN FOR A CONCERT, OR ALL SUMMER!

Musicians of any age who can read music, whether locals or summer visitors, are welcome to join the Band. Players can join for the entire season or just for one or two concerts, provided they attend at least one rehearsal prior.

Beginning in late June, we rehearse from 7:30-9 p.m. every Monday night at the Sailing Camp Park at 177 Barnes Road in Oak Bluffs. Concerts take place each Sunday from 8-9 p.m.

Vineyard Haven Band 1868
